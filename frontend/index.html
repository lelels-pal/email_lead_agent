Workflow

Built around the actual agent loop.

01

Read unread Gmail messages

The agent signs in, opens the inbox, and extracts sender, subject, and message body from the first unread email.

02

Evaluate buying intent

The lead evaluator returns a structured score, a conservative lead decision, concise reasoning, and a two-sentence reply draft.

03

Review and act

Operators can quickly validate the score, inspect the reply, and keep the workflow in draft mode for safer demos and internal use.

Features

Minimal surface area, strong operational signal.

Lead score visibility

Show lead quality without hiding the reasoning. Operators can tell at a glance why a message is worth follow-up.

Draft-first safety

Keep replies staged as drafts so teams can audit suggestions before the browser automation presses send.

Inbox-to-reply continuity

Queue, qualification, and reply composer stay in one interface so the review flow feels production-ready rather than experimental.

Demo-friendly presentation

The frontend mirrors a real operator console, which makes the project easier to demo, extend, and explain to collaborators.
